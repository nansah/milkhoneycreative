Web Design

Alia Li Design Studio

A full rebrand and product website redesign for a boutique interior and event design studio — built to attract premium clientele and drive consultation bookings.

Services Brand Identity, UX Design, Web Development
Category Product Website
Live Site alialidesigns.com

The Challenge

Alia Li was a talented interior and event designer with a portfolio of stunning work — but her digital presence didn't reflect it. The existing site felt generic, lacked clear client intake, and failed to communicate the luxury tier she was operating in.

  • No intake form to vet or qualify clients
  • Inconsistent branding that read more like a fashion blog than a design studio
  • Portfolio lacked quality imagery and didn't separate interior design from event design
  • No social proof or testimonials to build trust with prospective clients

The Approach

I executed a full rebrand starting with a visual identity that felt minimalist, editorial, and unmistakably high-end. From there I redesigned the site architecture to separate her two service lines — Interior Design and Event Design — giving each dedicated portfolio pages with their own visual treatment.

An intake form was integrated from the outset, so every inquiry comes pre-qualified. The copy was sharpened to speak directly to premium clientele and position Alia as a sought-after creative, not just a service provider.

Results

2× Growth in site visitors (91 → 194)
140% Increase in site sessions (94 → 225)
Higher Caliber of inquiries post-launch

Since launching the redesigned site, website users more than doubled and session volume increased 140% year-over-year. More importantly, the quality of inquiries improved — attracting the premium clientele Alia was designing for.
