Alia Li -Interior & Event Design Studio


Alia LI was seeking to rebrand her business starting with a minimalist and elegant website redesign to attract her target audience.



Services Provided:

    Branding
    Strategy
    UX Website Design & Development

Problem

Currently, the company is unable to vet clients and needs to implement an intake form

Company lacks testimonials and social proof and wants to increase trustworthiness

Not utilizing social media to generate leads as well

Portfolio lacks quality images and does not highlight her best work

Branding was inconsistent and seemed life a fashion blogger than an interior designer



Moodboard



Results

One of the company’s goals was to attract higher end clientele, so we elevated her branding to feel more luxury and professional while implementing an intake form to vet clients.

We also differentiated her work between Interior Design & Events Design with dedicated pages and portfolios for each

Since launching the site, website users have more than doubled from the previous year (91 to 194 site visitors) and site sessions have increased 140% from 94 to 225.
