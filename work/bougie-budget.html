The Project

This is a concept website designed for a financial coach whose audience is Black women ready to build wealth on their own terms. The goal was to create a digital experience that felt as empowering as the brand itself — modern, feminine, and unapologetically bold.

I wanted the site to feel vibrant and aligned with the boss babe aesthetic: a woman who is financially savvy, stylish, and knows her worth. Every design decision — from the color palette to the typography — was made with that woman in mind.

View Live Site

Design Goals

  • Create a warm, high-energy visual identity that resonates with the target audience.
  • Build a conversion-focused layout that drives ebook purchases, course enrollments, and webinar sign-ups.
  • Establish clear hierarchy and navigation so visitors can quickly find what they need.
  • Design a site that feels both luxurious and approachable — aspirational but not intimidating.
