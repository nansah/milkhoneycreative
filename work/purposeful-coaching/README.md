# Purposeful Coaching - Life Coach Branding and Website Design

## Client Overview

Website & Social Media Strategy and Redesign

When Renee approached me to help refine her Purposeful Coaching brand and website, she had a clear vision—she wanted her online presence to feel warm, inviting, and transformational while making it easy for potential clients to understand her offerings. However, like many entrepreneurs, her website and brand messaging weren’t fully aligned with her goals.

**Our mission:** To create a cohesive, user-friendly, and conversion-focused brand experience that reflects her personality while streamlining her coaching business.

**Services Provided:**

- Branding
- Social Media
- Web Design

## Branding & Design Strategy

At the heart of Renee’s brand is the belief that everything you need for transformation already lives within you — you just need the right guidance to unlock it. This philosophy needed to shine through in her visual identity, website layout, and messaging.

### Color Palette & Brand Feel

- **Green & Teal:** Symbolize transformation and personal growth.
- **Gold Accents:** Add warmth, refinement, and sophistication.
- **Pops of Vibrant Colors:** Keep the brand dynamic and engaging without feeling chaotic.

**Logo Symbols:**

- 🦋 Butterfly – Transformation and personal evolution
- 🌳 Tree – Grounded growth and pruning what no longer serves you

## Moodboard

A visual foundation for the rebranding, drawing inspiration from the brand’s essence and its target audience—women who aspire to a luxurious, elevated lifestyle while maintaining financial wisdom. The imagery, color palette, and styling reflect a balance of sophistication, confidence, and empowerment.

## Website Strategy & UX Enhancements

### The Challenge

Renee’s existing website was missing a clear client journey. Coaching services—her core offer—were hidden deep in the navigation, and visitors had to scroll extensively to find information. The homepage was primarily a newsletter signup form, rather than an introduction to her business.

### The Solution

- **Optimized Website Navigation:**
  - Home – Tagline, mission statement, coaching packages.
  - About – Personal story, deeper brand insights.
  - Services – Coaching packages, pricing, and booking options.
  - Blog – Repurposed newsletters and event recaps.
  - Events – Announcements for upcoming workshops & promotions.
  - Contact – Inquiry form for potential clients.
- **Homepage Redesign:**
  - Tagline & Mission Statement upfront.
  - Coaching services featured prominently.
  - Upcoming events & promotions visible.

## Email Marketing & Growth Strategy

- Transitioned to a dedicated ESP (ConvertKit/MailerLite) for automated email marketing.
- Implemented a lead magnet to encourage sign-ups.
- Repurposed newsletters into blog posts.
- Set up Google Analytics for tracking and optimization.

## Events & Community Engagement

- **Upcoming Events Page:** Lists all workshops & promotions.
- **Event Recaps on Blog:** Showcases past events with photos and testimonials.
- **Discount Banner:** Displays active promotions on the homepage.

**Major Events:**

- March 29th – Paint & Sip

## The Big Picture

By refining Renee’s brand strategy, website structure, and email marketing, we’ve positioned Purposeful Coaching for long-term success. Now, her online presence aligns with her mission—empowering individuals to uncover the transformation already within them.
