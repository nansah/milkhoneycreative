Web Design

Purposeful Coaching — Life Coach Branding & Website Design

Refining Renee's Purposeful Coaching brand and website to create a warm, inviting, and transformational online presence that aligns with her mission and drives client engagement.

Services Branding, Web Design, Social Media, Email Marketing

The Problem

Renee’s website and brand messaging weren’t fully aligned with her goals. The site lacked a clear client journey, coaching services were hidden, and the homepage was just a newsletter signup form.

  • Unclear navigation and client journey
  • Core services buried in navigation
  • Homepage not introducing the business

Branding & Design Strategy

At the heart of Renee’s brand is the belief that everything you need for transformation already lives within you — you just need the right guidance to unlock it. This philosophy shines through in her visual identity, website layout, and messaging.

  • Color Palette: Green & Teal (transformation, growth), Gold (warmth, sophistication), Vibrant accents (dynamic energy)
  • Logo Symbols: Butterfly (transformation), Tree (growth, groundedness)

The Solution

  • Optimized navigation prioritizing coaching services
  • Homepage with tagline, mission, and clear offers
  • Events and promotions featured up front
  • Conversion-focused content and layout

Growth & Community Engagement

  • Transitioned to a dedicated ESP for automation
  • Lead magnet for sign-ups
  • Repurposed newsletters into blog posts
  • Set up Google Analytics for tracking
  • Events page for workshops & promotions
  • Event recaps on blog
  • Discount banner for active promotions
  • Major events: Paint & Sip (March 29), Summer Beauty Event

Final Touches & Next Steps

  • Professional branding photoshoot
  • Google Form for testimonials
  • Social media integration

The Big Picture

By refining Renee’s brand strategy, website structure, and email marketing, we’ve positioned Purposeful Coaching for long-term success. Her online presence now aligns with her mission—empowering individuals to uncover the transformation already within them.
