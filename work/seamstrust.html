MVP & App Design

Seamstrust

A digital directory MVP solving a real problem: people within the African diaspora struggle to find trustworthy seamstresses. I designed and built it from concept through launch.

Services Product Strategy, UX Design, MVP Development
Category MVP & Web Application
Prototype View in Figma

The Problem

Growing up as a first-generation Ghanaian American, I frequently sought custom garments for special occasions — and consistently ran into the same wall. There was no reliable way to find a vetted seamstress in your area. Word-of-mouth was the only option, and it wasn't working.

As of 2022, African immigrants make up nearly 2M of the 4.6M Black immigrant population in the US — communities deeply connected to their cultural traditions and custom dressmaking. The market existed. The infrastructure didn't.

The Opportunity

  • No structured, searchable directory for African diaspora seamstresses existed
  • The only known competitor (Tailoring Needs) was defunct — partly due to charging customers to access the platform
  • Instagram, Google, and Etsy all had subpar discoverability for this use case
  • Trust was the core barrier — users needed verified profiles, portfolios, and reviews

Research & Discovery

I conducted 6 user interviews with women aged 18–45, US-based and of African descent, to understand their dressmaking process and pain points. Key questions included how they found seamstresses, what went wrong, and how they communicated throughout the process.

The research confirmed that trust was the primary concern. Users wanted to see a seamstress's experience, skills, portfolio, and verified reviews before committing — none of which existed in any current platform.

Design Process

Following the design thinking framework, I moved from discovery → user personas → journey mapping → low-fidelity wireframes → high-fidelity mockups → interactive prototype.

Two core users were defined: the prospective client (pre-event, searching for a seamstress) and the seamstress (seeking qualified leads). The directory was architected to serve both with dedicated flows for each.

The Solution

Seamstrust is a structured, searchable web application where users can find vetted seamstresses by location, specialty, and price range. Each seamstress has a full profile with portfolio images, reviews, and contact options — providing the social proof that was entirely missing from the market.
