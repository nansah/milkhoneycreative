About the Project

Stallion Fitness is a holistic lifestyle and fitness brand founded by Coach Jenn Eluwa. Her mission is to educate and inspire her community to adopt a healthier lifestyle and motivate them to live their best life!

She wants to rebrand, redesign her website and develop a media kit to solidify her identity as a holistic lifestyle brand and influencer. Taking this step in her business will demonstrate her impact and aid in securing brand partnerships and dedicated clientele.

Services Provided

  • UX Audit
  • Website Design
  • Rebranding

The Problem

Stallion Fitness' previous website was very difficult to navigate and read.

There was no clear CTA to book consultations and the process to book clients was very disjointed with several touchpoints including the website, scheduling via email and survey.

This was causing a decrease of sales and increase in bounce rate.

Results

200% Increase in pageviews
$200+ In ebook sales within first days of launch
New Brand deals secured post-launch

Stallion Fitness was able to garner a 200% increase in pageviews and sold over $200 in ebooks organically in the first few days of launching her site.
