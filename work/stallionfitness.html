Web Design

Stallion Fitness

A full brand and website overhaul for a holistic lifestyle and fitness brand — repositioned as a serious influencer and partnership target, with a digital presence to match.

Services UX Audit, Rebranding, Website Design, Media Kit
Category Brand & Product Website
Client Coach Jenn Eluwa

The Challenge

Coach Jenn Eluwa had built a genuine community around holistic fitness and lifestyle coaching — but her digital presence wasn't positioned to support the next level of her business. The existing website was hard to navigate, lacked clear calls to action, and the client intake process was fragmented across multiple touchpoints.

  • No clear CTA to book consultations on the existing site
  • Client booking required navigating the website, email, and a separate survey — a disjointed experience that lost leads
  • Branding lacked the polish needed to attract brand partnerships
  • No media kit to position Jenn as an influencer and partnership target

The Approach

Starting with a full UX audit, I identified the specific friction points driving bounce and lost conversions. Then I rebuilt the brand identity from the ground up — designing a visual system that communicated both the energy of fitness and the intentionality of holistic wellness.

The new website was architected around a single clear goal: drive consultation bookings. The intake flow was simplified to a single on-site form. A media kit was developed in parallel, purpose-built to present Jenn's audience data, brand partnerships, and story to potential collaborators.

The Result

200% Increase in pageviews
$200+ In ebook sales within first few days of launch
New Brand deals secured post-launch

The redesign delivered immediate results — 200% growth in pageviews and organic ebook sales within the first week. The media kit opened doors to brand partnerships that weren't accessible before, and the streamlined client intake process removed the friction that was costing her dedicated clients.
