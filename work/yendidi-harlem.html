The Project

Yendidi Harlem offers a line of organic herbal juices made with real ingredients and bold, familiar flavors. From refreshing sobolo to soothing lemon ginger, every bottle is crafted with intention — designed to nourish, restore, and bring people together.

I built their e-commerce store on Shopify, creating a shopping experience that reflects the brand's roots and energy. The design is clean, vibrant, and easy to navigate — making it simple for customers to discover the products and complete a purchase.
